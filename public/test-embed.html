Hiroo Chatbot Test Page

About This Page

This is a test page for the Hiroo Chatbot widget. The chatbot should appear as a floating button in the bottom-right corner of the page.

Click on the chat button to open the chatbot and test its functionality.

Note: This is a simplified version of the chatbot with no header or expand feature.
